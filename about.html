This is a game where you have to get to school, but nothing seems to go your way. Everything is going against you and you are tasked with many challenges to get School. You will have to push boulders out the way, battle bosses, make new friends and problem solve through locked doors. You will be able to aquire money called pogs in order to purchase items to help you reach the end goal of getting to school.

My name is Ryan Bischof and I am the creator of this game. I made it in class while attending York County School of Technology. Im 16 years old at the time of makeing this game and it was my first game I ever created. I hope to make many more games in the Future.
